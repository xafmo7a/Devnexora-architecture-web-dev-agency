Ideation & Conceptual

Nova Loom was envisioned as a UI/UX design project that reimagines how users interact with digital platforms by weaving clarity.

The name reflects its dual inspiration: “Nova,” symbolizing new light and discovery, and “Loom,” representing structures and the weaving of experiences. The visions was to an interface system that feels seamless, intuitivism, and immersive — like fabrics, carefully crafted thread by thread. Nova Loom set out to design experiences that are not just functional but emotionally engaging, guiding users effortlessly.

Production & Execution

The design approach for Nova Loom emphasized modularity, balance, and immersive detail.

Typography choices balanced modern sans-serifs with softer, humanized letterforms to improve readability and warmth. The color palette drew from gradients of cosmic tones deep blues, purples, and luminous highlights — reinforcing the “nova” inspiration.
